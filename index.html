Cell Mitosis Simulation Info:

- All the cells on the screen constantly grow until they reach their full size.
- Once the cell reaches its full size, you can click it so it performs mitosis
- Any cell currently performing mitosis will be highlighted and their chromozone size will double before splitting into two new cells.
- Mitosis can only occur when the cell is fully grown, hence why you can only click it when the cell is at its full size.
- Keep an eye on the cell count to see how quickly mitosis can increase the number of cells!
- Warning: The more cells around, the slower your computer will get so be careful.
